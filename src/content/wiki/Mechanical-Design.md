# Mechanical Design and 3D Models

The physical structure of Akku-Craft is what makes it "Modular". The housing is not just a box; it is a functional part of the thermal management and electrical insulation system.

## The Enclosure Concept

We use 3D-printable designs to allow for rapid iteration and local production. This aligns with our goal of decentralization — we don't need a factory to build Akku-Craft; we need a 3D printer and basic tools.

## Design Specifications

The models are optimized for FDM printing. We recommend using materials with high thermal resistance, such as PETG, ASA, or ABS, especially if the battery is expected to work under high loads. But PLA works fine for prototyping.

### Assembly Components

1. Main Frame: Holds the cell holders and the PCB.
2. Wall: Conceals the technology inside.

## Integration with Hardware

The internal layout is designed to minimize wire lengths. Short wires mean less resistance and less heat.
