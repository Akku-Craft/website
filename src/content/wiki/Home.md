# Akku-Craft: Sustainable • Modular • Decentralized

Welcome to the core documentation of Akku-Craft. We are building a future where battery systems are no longer disposable black boxes. Our mission is to reduce electronic waste by creating a modular battery ecosystem with decentralized monitoring.

Instead of throwing away entire battery packs when a single cell or component fails, Akku-Craft allows for repair, scalability, and granular control.

## The Vision
The current battery industry is plagued by planned obsolescence and proprietary designs. Akku-Craft breaks this cycle through three core pillars:
1. Sustainable: Designed for repairability and long lifecycles.
2. Modular: Scale your capacity by adding units, not by redesigning the system.
3. Decentralized: Intelligence is distributed across the system for better safety and reliability.

## How It Works
Akku-Craft is a synergy of three main domains:
- Hardware (Schematics): The physical circuit logic and power distribution.
- Mechanics (3D Models): The housing that keeps everything safe and portable.
- Firmware (ArduinoBMS): The "brain" that monitors voltage, current, and temperature to ensure safe operation.
